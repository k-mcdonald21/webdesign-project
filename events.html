Formula 1 on Twitch

In the absence of real Grand Prixs and a lack of any real racing, Formula 1 have taken measures to implement a "Virtual GP" to fill the void for racing fans the world over. This Virtual GP consists of many current F1 drivers, famous sports personalities and a number of content creators.
